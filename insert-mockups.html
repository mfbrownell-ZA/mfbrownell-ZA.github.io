Option C — Recommended

"This Phone is Mine"

Identity badge with mascot

TICKEYBOX

This phone belongs to:

Room No.

Stay connected. Stay safe.

What it is

  • Removes all dead UI — no numbered rows, no implied functionality
  • Ownership mechanic: kid writes their name = their phone
  • Mascot is a brand asset — can extend to packaging, app, merch
  • "Room No." mirrors the existing physical label slot on the handset
  • Works in any context: home, school, hospital, hotel
Recommended: Turns a functional gap into a brand asset. Strongest emotional resonance for 6–12 year olds. Lowest risk of confusing dead features.
