Model is robust to churn. More sensitive to PBX cost. Even at 25% churn the business remains profitable — the fixed cost base is low enough. The bigger swing is PBX cost: R20 vs R40 is a R480k annual delta at 2,000 units. Getting the formal rate card before the April pilot is the highest-priority open item for model reliability.
