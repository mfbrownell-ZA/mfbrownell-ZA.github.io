what works — build more of this

risk removal copy ("full refund. no questions.") → addresses checkout abandonment

production notes
copy rules + brand system

copy rules

all lowercase. always. no exceptions — not headlines, not CTAs, not the logo.
headline max: 60 characters. body max: 80 characters.
tone: playful irreverence meets no-bullshit clarity. science meets skincare.
never preachy. never corporate. no em dashes.
CTAs end in "." or "→" — not "!" or "NOW"
"befor." always with the full stop. never "befor" or "Befor."

copy patterns that convert

"1,200+ sa households switched this month"
"frizz isn't your hair type. it's your water."
"full refund. no questions. no drama."
"46% of sa water fails quality standards"
"r10 a day. less than your morning coffee."

copy patterns to avoid

"you've been showering wrong your whole life"
"KDF-55 media, calcium sulfite, zeolite ceramic"
"transform your bathroom experience"
"oh my word! I can't believe what came out of my filter"
shock/discovery + feature dumps + pure aesthetic = high hook rate, zero purchase rate. proven by data.
